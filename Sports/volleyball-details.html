Volleyball Match: CTU DB Dominate in Finals

Match Recap

CTU DB defeated USJR in a commanding 3-1 victory to claim the championship title in a thrilling volleyball showdown. From the very first serve, CTU DB showcased exceptional skill, teamwork, and determination, leaving the crowd in awe of their performance. Each set was a display of intensity and high-level play that kept fans on the edge of their seats.

Key Highlights: The team’s star spiker, Vince Ismael, dominated the game with a total of 25 kills, breaking through USJR’s defense with powerful and precise attacks. Setter Jason Riveral orchestrated the offense beautifully with pinpoint sets, enabling her teammates to shine. The libero, Justin Dumon, played an instrumental role in CTU DB's success, delivering jaw-dropping saves and maintaining the team's composure during critical moments. The fourth set proved decisive as CTU DB mounted a stunning comeback from a 20-15 deficit, ending the match with a dramatic ace serve by team captain Marco Montellano.

This victory marks CTU DB’s first championship in over a decade, making the triumph even sweeter. The win was celebrated with roaring applause and a standing ovation from their loyal fans, solidifying their legacy as one of the most formidable teams in the league.

Outstanding Players

Best Setter: Jason Riveral

Jason orchestrated the team's offense with exceptional precision, delivering 35 assists and creating multiple scoring opportunities.

Best Libero: Justin Dumon

Justin showcased incredible defensive skills, recording 18 successful digs and keeping the team alive during critical rallies.

Best Spiker: Vince Ismael

Vince was unstoppable at the net, delivering 20 successful spikes that broke through the opponent's defense repeatedly.

Best Scorer: Allan De Jesus

Allan led the team with 22 total points, contributing significantly through both spikes and serves.

Best Server: Marco Montellano

Marco's consistent serves, including 5 aces, kept the USJR on the defensive and disrupted their plays.
